Selahattin Cansiz

I am currently pursuing a PhD degree in Computer Science and Engineering under the supervision of Prof. Cigdem Gunduz-Demir at Koc University, Istanbul, Turkey. I am also a researcher at the KUIS AI Center at Koc University. My current research interests include medical image analysis using deep learning techniques.

From Jul. 2022 to Sep. 2024, I worked as an AI Engineer at PONS, a startup co-founded by Prof. Ilker Hacihaliloglu. During this time, our work focused on utilizing ultrasound imaging to enhance risk assessment and facilitate diagnosis at the point of care. As part of the research on ultrasound images, I participated as a visiting researcher at the University of British Columbia (UBC).

I received my MSc degree in Computer Engineering from Bilkent University, Ankara, Turkey, in 2022. As a research assistant under the supervision of Prof. Cigdem Gunduz-Demir, I focused on the analysis of retinal layers in OCT images.

I have a BSc degree in Industrial Engineering from Bogazici University, Istanbul, Turkey.
